These are my top 5 dream vacation zones

Nepal

Known for the majestic Himalayas, Nepal offers breathtaking landscapes and cultural heritage.

  • Hike to Everest Base Camp
  • Explore Kathmandu’s temples
  • Visit Chitwan National Park
  • Experience local cuisine
  • Go white-water rafting

Cinque Terre, Italy

A picturesque coastal region known for its colorful villages and stunning sea views.

  • Walk the Sentiero Azzurro
  • Swim in the Mediterranean
  • Try authentic Italian seafood
  • Visit historic churches
  • Enjoy sunset boat tours

South Island, New Zealand

Famous for its stunning landscapes, glaciers, and adventure sports.

  • Explore Milford Sound
  • Go bungee jumping in Queenstown
  • Visit Mount Cook National Park
  • See glowworm caves
  • Experience Maori culture

Iceberg Alley, Newfoundland

A unique destination where you can witness massive icebergs floating by.

  • Take an iceberg tour
  • Visit historical lighthouses
  • Go whale watching
  • Explore local fishing villages
  • Enjoy fresh seafood

Dominica

Known as the “Nature Island,” Dominica is a paradise for outdoor lovers.

  • Hike to Boiling Lake
  • Snorkel in Champagne Reef
  • Explore waterfalls
  • Visit the Kalinago Territory
  • Relax on black sand beaches
